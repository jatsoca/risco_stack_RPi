Holding registers (read-only):

  • Particiones regs 1-32 (uint16). 0=disarmed, 1=armed (away), 2=triggered, 3=Ready, 4=NotReady.
  • Zonas regs 33-544 (uint16). 0=cerrada, 1=abierta, 2=bypass.
  • Discrete inputs: bits 0-31 alarmada (1), bits 32-543 zona abierta (1).
